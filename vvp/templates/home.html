About Vivekanand Vidyapeeth

The Institution which came into being on 11-10-69 with an ambitious aim of imparting formal education to the wiling wards of a sizable section of the employees of Bhilai Steel Plant and others at least in their formative years through the medium of the mother tongue: Bengali has come of age. Our School was started in the month of October 1969 with two independent media of instruction [i.e. Bengali & English]. In the year 1980 our first batch, comprising of sixteen students, appeared in the Higher Secondary Examination of the MP Board. Our school is now run with the English medium section, Hindi being one of the compulsory subjects. Good citizens make a nation strong and the real strength lies in the development of character and intellectual power at all levels. This is what education is all about and this is the area in which we, in our modest way, tried to serve the community and the nation by establishing an institution after a hallowed name of "Swami Vivekananda"
